🌍 Popular Travel Destinations

Munnar

Munnar is famous for its tea plantations, waterfalls, cool climate, and scenic mountain views.

Ooty

Ooty is known as the Queen of Hills with beautiful lakes, gardens, toy trains, and pleasant weather.

Goa

Goa is famous for its sandy beaches, nightlife, Portuguese architecture, and water sports.

Kodaikanal

Kodaikanal offers lakes, pine forests, waterfalls, and breathtaking viewpoints perfect for nature lovers.
